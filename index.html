📖 How the scoring works — methodology
Bollinger Bands (BB)
Position in BB(20,2), bandwidth squeeze percentile, mid-line slope. High score = riding upper band with expanding bandwidth.
Volume Contraction (VCP)
Minervini setup: sequential price contractions (each tighter), volume drying up, proximity to 52-week high. High score = pivot-ready base.
Smart Money (SM$)
Accumulation/Distribution line, OBV slope, high-volume green vs red days. Catches institutional flow before price confirms.
Monthly Camarilla (CAM)
Levels (H1-H4, L1-L4, Pivot) computed from previous calendar month's H/L/C. Position above H3 = bullish bias for the month.
Bull / Bear Trap (TRAP)
Detects false breakouts: price breached recent high/low then closed back inside the range. Common F&O fake-out filter.
CRT + TBS
Candle Range Theory + Turtle Soup: identifies liquidity sweeps (price takes out a swing high/low) followed by reversal. ICT-style logic.
Breakout / Breakdown (B/O)
20-bar range tightness + distance to edge. Tight base + price hugging the top = imminent breakout candidate.
RSI Divergence
Compares price swings vs RSI(14) swings. Bullish div = price LL but RSI HL; bearish div = price HH but RSI LH. Plus OB/OS bias.
EMA Stack (Bonus)
20 > 50 > 200 stacked bullishly = strong trend. Built-in trend-confirmation filter.
Composite Score
Weighted sum of all indicators, normalized to ±100. ≥50 Strong Buy, 20–49 Buy, ±19 Neutral, −20 to −49 Sell, ≤−50 Strong Sell.
